2. Information the App Handles

The App may handle information that the user enters directly or that is generated while using the App, including:

  • Exercise names
  • Date-based workout counts and logs
  • App setting data such as language preference and dark mode preference

The current version of the App does not provide account registration, login, payment processing, advertising ID collection, or location tracking.

3. How Information Is Stored

Workout records entered by the user are stored locally on the user's device. The current version of the App does not use server-side account sync.

  • iOS: Data is stored in a local on-device database and may be included in iCloud device backups depending on the user's backup settings.
  • Android: Data is stored in a local on-device database and may be included in Android backup and restore depending on the user's Google backup settings.

This is operating system backup behavior, not real-time cloud synchronization.

4. Purpose of Processing

The App handles information only for the following purposes:

  • Recording and displaying workout counts
  • Providing daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly statistics
  • Maintaining app settings and core user experience
  • Supporting device-level backup and restore behavior

5. Third-Party Disclosure and External Transfers

In the current version, the App operator does not collect the user's workout records on a separate proprietary server and does not sell that data.

However, if the user enables device backup features, copies of device data may be handled under Apple or Google backup policies.

6. Retention and Deletion

Workout records may remain on the user's device until the user deletes them in the App or removes the App from the device.

  • The App includes a reset feature that deletes the history for the currently selected exercise.
  • Deleting the App may remove local data from the device, subject to operating system behavior.
  • Backup copies may remain for some time under Apple or Google backup policies.

7. User Choices

Users may directly take the following actions:

  • Edit exercise names
  • Edit or reset workout records
  • Delete the App
  • Manage iCloud backup or Google backup settings on the device

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children, and the operator does not intentionally provide features designed to collect children's personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if app features, operating policies, or applicable legal requirements change. Material changes may be announced through an app update or by replacing this page.
